How It Works

Let's try with Claude

Go to Claude → Settings → Connectors → Add Connectors and Add Records → Click Connect

Add Connectors

Go to settings in your AI client, click Add MCP Server, and input your Rulip connection details.

Test Activation

Verify your connection by sending a quick prompt to confirm your assistant can interact with Rulip.

Save & Sync

After every chat, simply tell the AI to save key details. They instantly sync across all your tools.

Security First

Designed with Security in Mind

Your privacy is our absolute priority. No one—not even us—can ever see your chats. We use local symmetric cryptographic encryption to lock your memories, keeping them 100% private.

Your raw chat data never reaches our servers. All encryption and storage occur entirely within your own private deployment context, making your memories mathematically unreadable to database administrators, hosts, or third parties. Only your authenticated AI client can decrypt and access them in real-time.
